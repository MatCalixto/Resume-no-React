Some of my Projects

Architect Portfolio Website

This project was made for an architect to display his portfolio, a little more about who he is as a professional in the About Me section as well as contact information in the end of the page.

The page is also responsive, in smaller screens, the navigation bar disappears, displaying a menu option in its place, allowing the page to be seen on wide screen devices or in smaller screen devices.

JavaScript was used, mainly to make the header invisible in the start of the page for a full screen immersive experience and to make the featured project image scroll slower than the rest of the page.
